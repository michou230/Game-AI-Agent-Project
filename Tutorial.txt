                                                 ===================GAMEPLAY RULES AND TUTORIAL====================

This is a turn based game, thus you get a turn to attack then it is the Enemy's turn.

The rules are simple:
Each character has a Normal Attack (na) , Skill (s) , Ultimate (u) and sometimes a Follow-Up Attack (fua).
Characters have energy to perform Ultimates and Skill Points to perform a Skill. All characters have HP, Shield, some also have ATK.

Normal Attack: Does low to moderate damage, restores one skill point and some energy.

Skill: Consumes one skill point per Skill performed and inflict Moderate to High damage, restores a good amount of energy.

Ultimate: Consumes 100 energy to unleash a powerful attack dealing huge damage.

Follow-Up Attack: A special attack that get triggered when certain conditions are met, its power and effects differ based on the character.

Energy is capped at a 100, skill points are capped at 5.

DOT (Damage Over Time) or Poison: At the start of each turn, sustain damage. The damage is 5% of the affected max hp. Bypasses shield.

Shield: If a character has a shield, when the character takes damage, they will not lose HP and lose shield instead. If the shield is not enough, reduce what is left to their HP.

Enemies: Have the same mechanics.  Enemies do NOT have skill points/energy restriction. When their hp falls under 40%, increase the chance to perform an ultimate by 25%.

Do note that these are the basic rules, each character has its own personalized skills and effects thus their own rules to play with.

Characters List provided for this game (5):
Mydei, Blade, Sparxie, Hyacine, Kafka.

Monsters list provided for this game (3):
Pollux, Silvermane Lieutenant, Harmonic Choir (Sunday).

============================================================ CHARACTERS ==================================================================

========== MYDEI ==========

SPECIAL TRAITS: This character consumes his own hp to inflict damage, heals, can be revived.

STATS: 5000 HP 

Normal Attack => "Vow Of Voyage": Inflicts damage based on 25% of his max hp.

Skill => "Deaths are Legion, Regrets are None": Consumes one skill point and 30% of his current hp to deal 40% dmg based on his max hp. If his current hp is less than 10% of his max hp, perform the attack but set his hp to 1.

Ultimate => "Throne of Bones": Deal 50% dmg and heal 20% based on his own max hp. If his hp was less than 10% when performing the ultimate, increase the dmg and heal by 100%.

Talent => "Blood for Blood": when Mydei is knocked down for the first time in battle, revive him with 30% of his max hp. This effect can only trigger once in battle.


========== BLADE ==========

SPECIAL TRAITS: This character consumes his own hp to inflict damage, heals.

STATS: 5000 HP 

Normal Attack => "Shard Sword": Inflicts damage based on 25% of his max hp.

Skill: => "Hellscape": Consumes one skill point to enter the Hellscape mode. Doesn't restore energy.
Hellscape mode: Upon entering the state, perform an Enhanced Attack, lasts for 2 extra turns. Cannot unleash skill while in this state.
Enhanced Attack: Consumes 25% of his current hp and increases the damage of the Normal Attack by 12% of his max hp. Doesn't restore skill points.

Ultimate: Set his hp to 50% of his max hp (hp might increase or decrease) and inflict 90% dmg of his max hp.

Talent: Whenever Blade loses hp by being attacked or from his own abilities, gain one stack of Charge. When Charge reaches 5, resets to 0 and unleash his Follow-Up ATK: "Shuhu's Gift" dealing 60% dmg and healing 25% hp based on his max hp.

========= SPARXIE ==========

SPECIAL TRAITS: This character consumes skill points to inflict damage based on her ATK. Increases the cap of Skill Points when using Sparxie to 8.

STATS: 5000 HP | 1500 ATK

Normal Attack => "Cat Got Your Flametoungue?": inflict 50% dmg of her max atk. Gains 2 Punchline.

Skill => "Boom! Sparxicle's Poppin'": Start the livestream in which sparxie harvests "Engagement Farming" where there is a 30% chance to trigger "Straight Fire" and a 70% chance to trigger "Unreal Banger", you can keep consuming Skill Points to trigger the effects up to 10 times to then unleash the Special Normal Attack "Bloom! Winner Takes All".
Straight Fire: gain 2 punchlines and 2 Skill Points
Unreal Banger: gain 1 punchline
Special Attack: Unleash a powerful attack based on 35% of Sparxie's base atk and the number of skills consumed (up to 10).

Ultimate => "Party's Wildin' and Cameras' Rollin'": Deals 150% of Sparxie's max atk. Gain 5 Punchlines.

Elation Skill => Signal Overflow: The Great Encore!: Every 3 turns unleash a powerful attack based on 100% of Sparxie's max ATK and the number of Punchlines accumulated. Each Punchline increases the dmg by 3%. Bypasses shield.

========== HYACINE ==========

SPECIAL TRAITS: This character heals, does damage based on her max hp and summons her pet Little Ica who deals dmg based on the accumulated healing tally. She can increase her max hp.

STATS: 5500 HP 

Normal Attack => "When Breeze Kisses Cirrus" : inflicts 15% dmg of her max hp.

Skill => "Love Over The Rainbow": Consumes 1 Skill Point to heal herself 15% hp of her max hp. Summons Little Ica.
Little Ica: has 50% of Hyacinthia's initial max hp and can perform his Special Attack when enough stacks are provided. If Hyacine's Skill was performed while Ica is on the field, Heals him for the same amount of hp too. Ica will remain on the field when summoned until he dies. While on the field, there is a 60% to target him instead of Hyacine. Cannot be poisoned.

Ultimate => "We Who Fly Into Twilight": Increases max hp by 25% of her own max hp for 3 turns and heals herself for 30% of her initial max hp. Summons Little Ica and perform his Special Attack and gain 3 Stacks. If Little Ica was present on the field when performing the Ultimate, heals him for the same amount of hp. Little Ica's max Hp is NOT affected.

Little Ica's Special Attack => "Rainclouds, Time To Go!" : Consume 1 Stack and deals damage to the enemy based on 50% of the accumulated healing tally whenever Hyacine performs a Normal Attack/Skill. Clears 50% of the tally for each time until the tally is less than 1000.

========== KAFKA ==========

SPECIAL TRAITS: This character does damage based on her atk and inflicts DOT (poison) on the enemy and has the ability to trigger it. Can increase her own ATK.

STATS: 4500 HP | 2000 ATK

Normal Attack => "Midnight Tumult" : inflicts 25% dmg of her ATK.

Skill => "Caressing Moonlight": Consumes 1 Skill Point to deal 40% of her ATK. If the opponent was poisoned upon unleashing this ability, trigger the DOT.

Ultimate => "Twilight Trill": Deals 90% of ATK and gain 2 Follow-Up Attack instances. If the opponent was poisoned upon unleashing this ability, trigger double the DOT.

Follow-Up Attack => "Gentle but Cruel": Deals 15% of her ATK and increase the Opponent's DOT stat by an additional 2. This ability can be unleashed whenever Kafka performs a Normal Attack or a Skill.

Talent: Whenever this character is attacked, increases her ATK by 2.5% of her original max ATK up to 25%.

============================================================ MONSTERS ==================================================================

========== POLLUX ==========

SPECIAL TRAITS: This enemy does damage based on their HP and inflicts DOT (Poison).

STATS: 20000 HP 

Normal Attack => "To Stay the Fallen" : inflicts 3% dmg of their max HP.

Skill => "To Die in Aromatic Pain": Steals 15% of your current HP. If your current HP is < than 500 reduce it to 1.

Ultimate => "To Bury the Slumbered": Poisons you for 2 turns and restore 10% of their max HP.

========== SILVERMANE LIEUTENANT ==========

SPECIAL TRAITS: This enemy does damage based on their ATK and reflect damage.

STATS: 10000 HP | 1300 ATK

Normal Attack => "Assault": Inflicts 30% dmg of their ATK.

Skill => "Pierce": Inflicts 70% dmg of their ATK and gains it as shield.

Ultimate => "Shield Reflect": Activates "Shield Reflect" for 3 turns.

Shield Reflect: Reduces damage received by 50% and launches a counter attack that deals 20% of their ATK.

========== HARMONIC CHOIR (SUNDAY) ==========

SPECIAL TRAITS: This enemy has two phases and inflicts dmg based on their ATK.

STATS: STATS: 13000 HP | 1500 ATK

PHASE 1:

Normal Attack => "Maestoso": Inflicts 20% dmg of their ATK.

Skill => "Tempestoso": Inflicts 50% dmg of their ATK.

Ultimate => "Come Un Sogno": Summons 2 Echoes. If Echoes are already summoned, restores the Echoes HP to max.

Echoes: They have 1000 HP. Each Echo present on the field increases Sunday's ATK by 10%. When attacking, there is an 80% chance to attack them instead of Sunday, and each defeated Echo restores 1000 HP to Sunday and 500 to the Player. If Sunday was attacked while there are any Echos on the field, defeat them instantly.

PHASE 2:

Upon defeating the first phase, restore 1500 HP to the player. Sunday enters "Embryo of Philosophy" state. If he was poisoned, clear it.

STATS: 20000 HP | 1500 ATK

Embryo of Philosophy: Reduces damage received by 70% and the Player gains a shield based on 50% of the initial damage they caused.

Special Attack => "Im Anfang war die Tat": After 7 turns, unleash a very powerful attack based on 500% of their ATK.
